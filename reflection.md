# Reflection: Profile Comparisons

## Chill/Lofi vs. Hype/Workout EDM

The Chill/Lofi profile returned lofi songs in all three top spots because the catalog has three lofi tracks that all matched the genre and mood label exactly. The Hype/Workout EDM profile only had one EDM song in the catalog, so after "Drop the Beat" took the top spot, the system filled positions two through five with pop and latin songs that had similar energy and tempo numbers. When a genre has many songs, the top results feel consistent but when it has only one song then the system falls back on numerical similarity and mixes in songs from other genres.

## Melancholic Blues vs. Adversarial

Both profiles share the same genre and mood labels so they both received "Back Porch Blues" at number one and "River of Tears" at number two. The difference starts at position three. The Melancholic profile had a low energy target so the rest of the top 5 filled with quiet folk and classical songs that had similar low energy values. The Adversarial profile had a high energy target, so positions three through five were taken by an EDM track, a rock track, and a pop track. A user who wants the feel of blues but the energy of a workout playlist will always get a mixed result that does not fully satisfy either preference.
